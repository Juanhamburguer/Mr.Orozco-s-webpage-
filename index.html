CUPHEAD

What is Cuphead about? Two adventurous cugs, one named Cuphead, and the other Mugman. The two of them were out for a walk in the city, until they saw the Casino, and they went to bet. After betting, a mysterious person arrives, who was called "The Devil". Cuphead and Mugman decided to bet their souls with the devil for a great reward, The Devil accepted. Some time later, Cuphead and Mugman lost the bet, they had repented, they began to beg to The Devil not to take their souls. The Devil was thinking, and decided to give them a chance, but with one condition, they must bring the contracts of all the villains from the Inkwell Islands. Cuphead and Mugman agreed, then The Devil kicked them out of the Casino, Cuphead and Mugman started getting the contracts.
The villain contracts can be obtained after defeating the villains of Inkwell Isle I, II, and III, but if you have the DLC (The Delicious Last Course) it will be different, but it will be the same missions in Inkwell Isle IV

When was Cuphead released and who created it?

September 29, 2017. The game was created by Studio MDHR.

The designer of Cuphead is called: "Jared Moldenhauer".

Cuphead is the first video game from Studio MDHR, an indie developer studio formed by the Moldenhauer brothers. Their development began in 2010, working from their respective homes in Toronto and Saskatchewan.

Even though Cuphead is older than his younger brother Mugman, Mugman is much smarter than Cuphead, because Cuphead just risks everything without thinking what might happen, while Mugman thinks what he's going to do in a smart way.
